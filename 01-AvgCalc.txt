Q: Algorithm that calculates the average of two integers received from the user and shows it on the screen

Step 1 - Get Started
Step 2 - Data Entry: Enter the first integer (number1)
Step 3 - If number1 is an integer, go to step 4
Otherwise, write it on the screen (Please enter an integer!) and go to Step 2.
Step 4- Data Entry: Enter the second integer (number2)
Step 5 - If number2 is an integer, go to step 6
Otherwise, write it on the screen (Please enter an integer!) and go to Step 4.
Step 6- Operation and assignment: average = (number1 + number2) / 2
Step 7 - Print: average
Step 8 - Exit
